Pancake

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sunflower
  • Maple syrup

Instructions

  1. Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk and 1 tbsp sunflower into a bowl then whisk to a smooth batter.
  2. Set a medium greased frying pan over a medium heat.
  3. Cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden.
  4. Serve it with maple syrup on it.
