French Toast

Home Recipes Contact

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tbsp of ground cinnamon
  • 4 slices of hearty sandwich bread

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg, vanilla and cinnamon in a shallow dish and stir in milk.
  2. Dip the bread slices in the egg mixture.
  3. Cook the bread slices on lightly greased griddle.
